Get help with housing from

If you are renting

You can apply for a one-off payment to help you if you are struggling with your rent.

This is called a Discretionary Housing Payment.

To qualify, you must already be getting housing benefit or the housing part of Universal Credit.

Apply on your council's website

Apply for a council house

Anyone can apply for a council house from . You do not need to be from the area of have family here.

You will get priority if you:

  • are already living here
  • have family living here
  • have a job here

You can apply directly on your council's website and look at their housing register information pack if you need more help.

Help with homelessness

The council must help you if you are homeless now or might be in the next 56 days.

Depending on your circumstances, you could get some general advice, temporary accommodation or a longer-term place to stay.

Go to the housing office

It is best to go directly to the housing office and get there as early as you can. You may have to wait to be seen.
